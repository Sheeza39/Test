Meeting Rooms

Daftarkhwan is equipped with luxurious multi-purpose meeting spaces. Our meeting space varies in size, from a meeting room for four to a state of the art conference room for ten and expansive boardrooms with brilliant views. Our easily-bookable meeting space is available to both our community of members and external clients. We provide you with all the tools you need to conduct a successful meeting. From glass whiteboards and high speed internet to Smart TVs for virtual participants and presentations, join our meeting space and amplify your team’s collaboration. Impress and host investors and clients in a professional meeting space, giving your business additional credibility. From when they first step foot into the office, to front desk services and refreshments, we’ve got you covered. Run the smoothest meetings by booking meeting space hours from our easy to use app, showing real-time availability or by simply calling reception if you’re already a member.
